## 1 · Spaghetti Bolognese

**Estimated cost** $2.76 | **Time** ≈ 35 min [bls.gov](https://www.bls.gov/regions/mid-atlantic/data/averageretailfoodandenergyprices_usandmidwest_table.htm)[walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/ip/Great-Value-Diced-Tomatoes-with-Green-Peppers-Celery-Onions-14-5-oz-Can/45210759?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

### Ingredients – 1 plate

- 100 g spaghetti
- 125 g lean ground beef
- 50 g onion (≈ ¼ med)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 200 g canned diced tomatoes
- 1 Tbsp tomato purée
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

### In-depth steps

1. **Prep** Dice onion; mince garlic. Bring a 4 qt pot of salted water to a rolling boil.
2. **Sauté base** Heat oil in a deep skillet (med-high). Sweat onion 3 min; add garlic 30 sec.
3. **Brown beef** Add mince; cook 5 min, breaking up until browned.
4. **Build sauce** Clear a hot spot, toast tomato purée 60 sec. Stir in tomatoes, oregano & 60 ml water; scrape fond. Simmer gently 15 min.
5. **Cook pasta** Boil spaghetti 8–9 min to al dente; reserve 60 ml cooking water, drain.
6. **Marry** Season sauce, loosen with reserved water as needed, toss in pasta 1 min until glossy. Serve hot.

## 3 · Oven-Baked Mac & Cheese

**Estimated cost** $1.82 | **Time** ≈ 30 min [bls.govbls.gov](https://www.bls.gov/regions/mid-atlantic/data/averageretailfoodandenergyprices_usandmidwest_table.htm)

### Ingredients – 1 baking-dish serve

- 100 g macaroni
- 15 g unsalted butter
- 1 Tbsp all-purpose flour (~ 8 g)
- 250 ml whole milk
- 80 g grated cheddar
- 15 g breadcrumbs
- Salt & pepper

### In-depth steps

1. **Par-boil** Cook macaroni 7 min; drain.
2. **Roux** Melt butter, whisk in flour 1 min until nutty.
3. **Béchamel** Whisk in milk gradually; simmer 4 min to nappe thickness.
4. **Cheese** Off heat, melt in 60 g cheddar; season.
5. **Combine** Fold pasta into sauce; pour into buttered 20 cm dish.
6. **Top & bake** Mix remaining cheddar with crumbs, scatter. Bake 15 min @ 200 °C/400 °F until bubbling & golden. Rest 5 min before serving.

## 10 · Egg Fried Rice

**Estimated cost** $1.12 | **Time** ≈ 15 min

### Ingredients – 1 bowl

- 150 g cooked white rice (~1 cup cooked)
- 2 medium eggs
- 50 g frozen mixed veg (peas/carrots/corn)
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce (~15 ml)
- 1 tsp oil for pan

### In-depth steps

1. **Heat pan** over medium-high, add oil.
2. **Veg**: Add frozen veg, stir-fry 2–3 min.
3. **Eggs**: Push veg aside, crack in eggs. Scramble gently till set.
4. **Combine**: Add cooked rice, break clumps. Drizzle soy sauce evenly.
5. **Toss & heat through**: Stir-fry 2–3 min until piping hot.
6. **Serve** hot, optionally garnish with sliced green onion.

## 25 · Sheet-Pan Sausage & Veg

**Estimated cost** $ 2.14 / plate | **Time** ≈ 25 min

[carrollssausage.com](https://www.carrollssausage.com/link-sausage-smoked-per-pound.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com)[download.bls.gov](https://download.bls.gov/pub/time.series/ap/ap.series?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

### Ingredients

- 1 smoked sausage link (≈ 100 g)
- ½ bell pepper, 2 cm chunks (≈ 80 g)
- ½ courgette/zucchini, half-moons (≈ 80 g)
- 40 g red onion wedges
- 1 tsp olive oil, pinch dried herbs, salt & pepper

### In-depth steps

1. **Pre-heat** oven to 200 °C / 400 °F; line a small sheet pan.
2. **Toss veg** with oil, herbs, salt & pepper; scatter on pan.
3. **Add sausage** — slice link into 2 cm pieces; nestle among veg.
4. **Roast** 15 min; shake pan, roast 8-10 min more until veg are char-edged and sausage reads 74 °C.
5. **Serve** straight from the tray or over quick couscous.
