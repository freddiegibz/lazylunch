## 1 · Greek Yogurt Berry Bowl

- **Estimated cost** · **$2.62 / serving**
- **Time** · Quick (≈ 5 min)

### Ingredients

- 200 g Greek yogurt
- 100 g mixed berries
- 1 Tbsp honey
- *Optional* granola or seeds

### Steps

1. Rinse & drain berries.
2. Spread yogurt in bowl.
3. Top with berries.
4. Drizzle honey (add crunch if desired).
5. Serve at once.

## 2 · Avocado Toast with Egg

- **Estimated cost** · **$2.98 / serving**
- **Time** · Quick (≈ 10 min)

### Ingredients

- 2 slices whole-grain bread
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 eggs
- Pinch salt (+ pepper / chili flakes)

### Steps

1. Toast bread.
2. Halve, pit & mash avocado with salt.
3. Fry eggs to preference.
4. Spread avocado over toast; crown each slice with an egg.
5. Season & serve hot.

## 3 · Banana Oat Pancakes

- **Estimated cost** · **$1.36 / serving**
- **Time** · Medium (≈ 10 min)

### Ingredients

- 1 ripe banana
- 100 g rolled oats
- 2 eggs

### Steps

1. Blend banana, oats & eggs until smooth.
2. Cook 8-10 cm pancakes (2-3 min per side).
3. Repeat until batter is finished.
4. *Optional* fruit, yogurt or honey toppings.

## 4 · Overnight Oats

- **Estimated cost** · **$0.89 / serving**
- **Time** · Overnight soak (prep < 5 min)

### Ingredients

- 50 g rolled oats
- 150 ml milk (dairy or alt)
- 1 Tbsp chia seeds
- Handful fresh berries (+ honey or nut butter)

### Steps

1. Combine oats, chia & milk in a jar; stir.
2. Refrigerate ≥ 6 h.
3. In the morning, loosen & top with berries (plus optional drizzle).
4. Enjoy cold.

## 5 · Smoked Salmon & Cream Cheese Bagel

- **Estimated cost** · **$6.26 / serving**
- **Time** · Quick (≈ 5 min)

### Ingredients

- 1 plain bagel
- 50 g cream cheese
- 70 g cold-smoked salmon
- Lemon wedge (+ dill / pepper)

### Steps

1. Split & toast bagel.
2. Spread cream cheese evenly.
3. Layer salmon slices.
4. Squeeze lemon, add herbs/pepper.
5. Serve immediately.
